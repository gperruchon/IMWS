Expertise

When life changes, banking must too.

Our work is most impactful at life inflection points — when the stakes are high and an independent voice is essential.

Life Scenarios

Eight situations. One independent advisor.

Each scenario below is one we navigate regularly — structuring a clear, protected path forward.

⚖️

Divorce & Asset Separation

When shared accounts and joint mandates must be untangled, we help define which institution serves each party's future needs — and negotiate the transition with full confidentiality.

🏛️

Inheritance & Estate Transition

Inheriting a banking relationship is not the same as choosing one. We audit the existing setup, assess suitability for the heir's profile, and manage the transition with minimal disruption.

🕊️

Widowhood — Banking Continuity

The surviving spouse often inherits banking arrangements they never chose and may not understand. We provide clarity, independence, and a structured review at a time when simplicity matters most.

🏢

Business Sale — Private Banking Setup

A significant liquidity event demands a private banking setup that reflects new realities. We run a full market process to identify the right partners for significant, newly liquid assets.

🏦

Corporate Treasury — Partner Selection

Family offices and SMEs holding idle treasury need banking partners optimised for yield, security and flexibility. We identify and negotiate with suitable institutions.

✈️

Expatriation — New Banking Setup

Relocating internationally means rethinking every banking relationship. We help new arrivals in Switzerland — and Swiss residents moving abroad — build a banking structure that fits their new reality.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Intergenerational Wealth Transfer

Transmitting assets across generations requires banking partners who understand long-term governance and succession planning. We ensure the structure is ready before the transfer happens.

📊

Investment Mandate Review

Is your portfolio mandate still appropriate? Are your managers delivering? We run an independent performance and cost review — and identify concrete improvements to structure or providers.
