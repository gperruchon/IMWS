The Team

Independent by design.

A boutique advisory practice intentionally built without institutional conflicts — structured to serve families, not banks.

GP

Gaylord Perruchon

Founder & Principal Advisor

Gaylord Perruchon is an independent wealth management consultant based in Geneva with over a decade of experience working inside and alongside the Swiss private banking industry. He founded IM Wealth Select® after recognising that most high net worth families lack an objective counterpart when choosing, reviewing, or renegotiating their banking relationships.

Having worked as a private banker himself, Gaylord understands how mandates are structured, how fees are layered, and where conflicts of interest most often arise. His advisory model is deliberately boutique: each client receives direct access, full continuity, and a relationship that is never passed to a junior team.

IM Wealth Select® operates exclusively on a flat-fee basis. No retrocessions. No referral income. No asset-linked remuneration.

Our Principles

Three commitments we never compromise.

Independence

No institutional affiliation

We are not owned by, affiliated with, or compensated by any bank, insurance group or investment manager. Our only financial relationship is with you.

Transparency

Full fee disclosure

Our engagement letters specify every cost before we begin. No success fees. No hidden charges. No incentive to recommend one institution over another.

Continuity

Gaylord Perruchon handles every mandate personally. Your relationship is never delegated to an associate. You call the person who does the work.
