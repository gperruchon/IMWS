What we collect

Personal data we collect.

Website access data

Our web server automatically records the following technical data each time a page is requested:

  • IP address of the requesting device
  • Date and time of the request
  • Pages and resources requested
  • Referring website (if applicable)
  • Browser type and operating system
  • Technical server and error logs

Contact-form & correspondence data

When you submit the contact form or write to us directly, we collect:

  • Full name (first and last)
  • Email address
  • Country of residence
  • Approximate investable assets range

Please do not send sensitive documents — such as account statements, identification documents or custody reports — through the contact form. Once an engagement is confirmed we will provide a secure, encrypted channel for document exchange.

Use, disclosure & transfers

Why we process data, and who may see it.

We process personal data for the following purposes: to operate and secure this website; to respond to enquiries submitted via the contact form; to assess whether our services are relevant to your situation; and to comply with applicable legal, regulatory and tax obligations. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling or automated decision-making.

Our legal basis for processing contact-form and correspondence data is our legitimate interest in responding to prospective client enquiries and, where a mandate is engaged, the performance of a contract. Website access data is processed under a legitimate-interest basis for security and proper functioning of the site. We will never sell, rent or share your data with banks, investment managers or any other financial intermediary without your explicit, specific written instruction.
