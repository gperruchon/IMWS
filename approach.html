Our Approach

A rigorous, independent process.

01 — Diagnose 02 — Select & Negotiate 03 — Monitor Fees

Step 01

Diagnose.

Know exactly where you stand.

We define your objectives and risk profile, then audit your current banking relationships to identify what is aligned, what is costly, and what should be challenged.

  • Investment goals and personal objectives
  • Risk profile, liquidity needs and time horizon
  • Current banks, mandates, asset allocation and holdings
  • Performance versus objectives and relevant benchmarks
  • Full fee review: management, custody, transaction costs and retrocessions
  • Risk exposure, reporting transparency and service quality

Deliverable

Investment Profile & Banking Audit

A clear diagnostic of your objectives, current setup, key gaps and priorities — mandate by mandate.

Duration

1 – 3 weeks

Depending on the number of banking relationships, portfolio complexity, and availability of documentation.

Step 02

Independent Selection & Negotiation.

Choose providers on merit, not relationships.

For each mandate, we run a dedicated tender across eligible providers, compare proposals side by side, and negotiate fees and terms on your behalf.

  • One dedicated tender / Request for Proposal (RFP) per mandate
  • Screening of eligible private banks, External Asset Managers (EAMs) and specialist providers
  • Side-by-side comparison of proposals, fees, service, access and reporting
  • Final selection made together with you
  • Negotiation of fees, terms and service commitments
  • Transition support and implementation coordination

Deliverable

Mandate Tender Report

A clear comparison of shortlisted providers, negotiated terms and our recommendation for each mandate.

Duration

2 – 4 weeks

We run a formal market process — obtaining proposals, clarifying terms, negotiating, and presenting you with a clear recommendation.

Step 03

Monitor.

Keep the mandate accountable.

After onboarding, we monitor whether each mandate stays aligned with your objectives — and help you act when standards slip.

  • Performance and risk reviewed against agreed objectives
  • Verification of fees, costs and negotiated terms
  • Mandate compliance, reporting quality and service review
  • Access to an online reporting tool, with consolidation where relevant
  • Quarterly review meeting and clear action points
  • Trigger-based re-tendering when standards are no longer met

Deliverable

Governance Report & Online Reporting Access

Regular governance reporting, an online reporting tool, quarterly reviews, and clear recommendations when needed.

Format

First 12 months after onboarding

Included for the first 12 months after onboarding; continued governance can then be maintained at a reduced annual rate.

Transparent Fees

No retrocessions. No referral fees. Paid only by you.

Our fees are largely offset by the savings negotiated with your bank within the first 12 months. We receive no commissions from banks — ever.
