Our Approach

A rigorous, independent process.

Know exactly where you stand.

We define your objectives and risk profile, then audit your current banking relationships — identifying what is aligned, what is costly, and what should be challenged.

Deliverables & Timeline

Deliverable

Investment Profile & Banking Audit

Mandate by mandate diagnostic of gaps and priorities.

Duration

1 – 3 weeks

Depending on number of relationships and complexity.

  • Investment goals, risk profile and time horizon
  • Current banks, mandates and asset allocation
  • Performance vs objectives and benchmarks
  • Full fee review: management, custody and retrocessions

Choose on merit, not relationships.

For each mandate, we run a dedicated tender across eligible wealth managers, compare proposals side by side, and negotiate fees and terms on your behalf.

Deliverables & Timeline

Deliverable

Mandate Tender Report

A ranked comparison of the best-fit providers for your profile and mandate.

Duration

2 – 4 weeks

Formal market process — proposals, negotiation, recommendation.

  • One RFP per mandate across eligible private banks
  • Side-by-side comparison of fees, service and reporting
  • Final selection made together with you
  • Transition support and implementation coordination

Keep the mandate accountable.

After onboarding, we monitor whether each mandate stays aligned with your objectives — and act when standards slip.

Deliverables & Timeline

Deliverable

Governance Report & Online Reporting

Quarterly reviews and clear recommendations when needed.

Duration

First 12 months included

  • Consolidated online reporting across all providers
  • Trigger-based re-tendering when standards slip
