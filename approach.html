Our Approach

A rigorous, independent process.

Three structured steps that cover every dimension of your private banking relationship — from initial diagnosis to long-term governance.

01 — Audit 02 — Select 03 — Monitor Fees

Step 01

Precision Audit.

Before making any recommendation, we build a complete, factual picture of your current banking setup. This is not a superficial review — it is a forensic analysis of every dimension of your relationship.

  • Full cost mapping: management fees, custody fees, transaction fees, retrocessions
  • Portfolio risk assessment and asset allocation review
  • Reporting quality and transparency evaluation
  • Service responsiveness and relationship quality
  • Mandate performance vs. benchmark and stated objectives

Deliverable

Banking Situation Report

A clear, confidential document summarising your current situation, identified gaps, and the potential annual saving from restructuring.

Duration

2 – 4 weeks

Depending on the number of banking relationships, portfolio complexity, and availability of documentation.

Deliverable

Comparative Selection Report

A structured comparison of shortlisted private banks and wealth managers, with negotiated term sheets and our recommendation.

Duration

4 – 8 weeks

We run a formal market process — obtaining proposals, clarifying terms, negotiating, and presenting you with a clear recommendation.

Step 02

Independent Selection & Negotiation.

We conduct an independent market process on your behalf, drawing on our network and analytical tools to identify the institutions and mandates that genuinely match your profile.

  • Identification of suitable private banks, EAMs and specialist providers
  • Structured RFP (Request for Proposal) process via SmartRFP
  • Fee and terms negotiation — we advocate for you, not the bank
  • Side-by-side comparison across service, cost, access and reporting
  • Transition support and implementation coordination

Step 03

Active Governance.

A banking relationship is not a one-time decision. Over time, fees drift, mandates diverge, and service quality erodes. We provide the ongoing oversight that protects your interests year after year.

  • Quarterly performance reviews against agreed benchmarks
  • Annual fee verification — ensuring negotiated terms are honoured
  • Mandate compliance monitoring — is your bank doing what it promised?
  • Bi-annual review meetings with your advisors
  • Trigger-based re-tendering if standards fall below thresholds

Deliverable

Governance Dashboard & Reports

Quarterly summaries, annual cost verification, and a clear escalation protocol if any partner underperforms.

Format

Annual retainer

Ongoing monitoring is structured as a flat annual fee — fully independent, no performance fees, no asset-linked remuneration.

Transparent Fees
